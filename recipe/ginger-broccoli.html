Ingredients

Yield: 1 Broccoli

Prep Time: 5min

Cooking Time: 15min

Total Time: 20min

  • Broccoli 1 head
  • Ginger 1 thumb
  • Olive Oil 3tbsp

Directions

  1. Cut the broccoli into small florets.
  2. Finely chop or shave the ginger
  3. Toss the broccoli and ginger in olive oil.
  4. Place on a baking tray and bake at 220Â°C for 15 minutes or until slightly browned.
  5. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  6. Enjoy!

Tip!


You can add some sesame oil for more flavor.

Be careful, the broccoli can burn very easily!
